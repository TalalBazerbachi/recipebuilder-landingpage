================================================================================

Label: Cost Breakdown
Heading: Full Cost Breakdown for Every Recipe
Subtitle: RecipeBuilder breaks down the cost of every recipe into its individual components. You see the total yield, total cost, and per serving cost at a glance — so you always know exactly what each portion costs to produce.

Total Yield & Total Cost:
Every recipe displays its total yield in grams alongside the total cost in AED. As you add or adjust ingredients, the figures update automatically. No spreadsheets, no manual calculations — just accurate numbers every time you open a recipe.

Per Serving Cost & Serving Size:
Define your serving size in grams and RecipeBuilder divides the total cost by the number of servings to give you the per serving cost in AED. This is the number that matters most when you set menu prices and evaluate whether a recipe makes financial sense for your business.

INGREDIENT COSTS — "Track the Cost of Every Ingredient"
================================================================================

Label: Ingredient Costs
Heading: Track the Cost of Every Ingredient
Subtitle: Every ingredient in your recipe carries a cost. RecipeBuilder lets you assign a price per unit to each ingredient and automatically sums them up based on the quantities used. When supplier prices change, update the ingredient cost once and every recipe that uses it recalculates instantly.

How Ingredient Cost Tracking Works:

1. Add ingredients to your recipe — search from a database of over 20,000 FDA-sourced ingredients with complete nutritional profiles, or create custom ingredients with your own data.

2. Set the cost per unit in AED — assign a price to each ingredient based on what you pay your suppliers. Costs are stored and reused across all recipes that share the same ingredient.

3. Specify the quantity used — enter the exact amount of each ingredient for your recipe. RecipeBuilder multiplies the unit cost by the quantity to calculate the ingredient line cost.

4. View the total food cost — all ingredient line costs are summed into the total food cost for the recipe, displayed prominently in AED alongside the per serving breakdown.

COMPLETE COST PICTURE — "Four Cost Categories That Cover Everything"
================================================================================

Label: Complete Cost Picture
Heading: Four Cost Categories That Cover Everything
Subtitle: Ingredient cost alone does not tell the full story. RecipeBuilder captures four distinct cost categories so your per serving cost reflects the true expense of getting a finished product to your customer.

1. Food Cost
   Track raw ingredient costs for every recipe automatically. Each ingredient carries its unit price, and RecipeBuilder calculates the total food cost based on the exact quantities used in your recipe.

2. Labor Cost
   Factor in the labor required to prepare each recipe. Assign labor cost per recipe to capture the true cost of production, from prep work to plating and packaging.

3. Packaging Cost
   Include the cost of containers, labels, bags, boxes, and any other packaging materials. Packaging cost is added per recipe so your total cost reflects every expense before the product leaves your kitchen.

4. Wastage Cost
   Account for ingredient waste, trim loss, and shrinkage. Adding a wastage cost ensures your per serving cost reflects real-world production conditions, not just theoretical ingredient amounts.

Label: Pricing & Profit
Heading: From Selling Price to Net Profit in One View
Subtitle: Set your selling price, configure VAT, account for delivery platform commissions, and see your net profit percentage — all on one screen, all in AED. RecipeBuilder removes the guesswork from food business pricing.

1. Selling Price (VAT Exclusive & Inclusive)
   Set your selling price and see both the VAT-exclusive and VAT-inclusive amounts side by side. RecipeBuilder handles the arithmetic so you can price your products accurately for menus, retail shelves, and delivery platforms.

2. Configurable VAT Percentage
   Configure the VAT rate that applies to your market. Whether you operate in the UAE at 5% VAT or in a jurisdiction with a different rate, RecipeBuilder lets you set the exact percentage and recalculates all pricing instantly.

3. Aggregator Commission Tracking
   Selling through food delivery aggregators? Enter the commission percentage charged by each platform and RecipeBuilder deducts it from your revenue to show you what you actually keep after every sale.

4. Net Profit Percentage
   After accounting for food cost, labor, packaging, wastage, VAT, and aggregator commissions, RecipeBuilder calculates your net profit percentage and highlights it in green. One number that tells you if the recipe is financially viable.

WHY IT MATTERS — "Cost Visibility That Drives Better Decisions"
================================================================================

Label: Why It Matters
Heading: Cost Visibility That Drives Better Decisions

Stop Guessing, Start Knowing:
Many food businesses price their products based on intuition or rough estimates. RecipeBuilder replaces guesswork with precise, itemized cost data. When you can see that a recipe costs 12.40 AED to produce and your selling price is 35 AED, you make decisions from a position of clarity — not hope.

Catch Margin Erosion Early:
Supplier prices fluctuate. When the cost of a key ingredient increases, it can silently eat into your profit margin. RecipeBuilder surfaces these changes immediately. Update an ingredient price and you see the impact on every recipe that uses it, so you can adjust selling prices or find alternatives before margins erode.

Price Confidently Across Channels:
Selling through your own shop, a delivery aggregator, and a retail partner each involves different cost structures. Aggregators take a commission; retail may require different packaging. RecipeBuilder lets you model each scenario with its commission and cost inputs so you can set channel-specific prices that protect your margins everywhere you sell.
